Our Story

"I noticed a gap in Kenya's plushie market - either expensive new ones or worn-out thrifted options. That's when I decided to make cute, quality plushies accessible to everyone. Because everyone deserves a cuddly companion to share their journey with."

- Malik, Founder of Lainika

The Inspiration

At just 20 years old, I saw how plushies brought joy to people's lives - couples celebrating their love, parents nurturing their children's imagination, and individuals finding comfort in their own space. But quality plushies were either too expensive or hard to find. That's when Lainika was born.

Our Mission

We believe that every moment of joy deserves to be celebrated - whether it's your first date, anniversary, birthday, or just because. Our plushies are more than just toys; they're companions for life's precious moments, bringing warmth to your relationships and spaces.

Our Promise
